**Project Overview**
The Bike Sales dataset provides insights into customer demographics and purchasing patterns for a retail company specializing in bicycles. The objective of this project is to analyze sales trends, customer preferences, and key performance metrics using Excel tools and data visualization techniques. The dataset helps identify high-value customer segments, understand buying behavior, and support business decisions related to marketing and inventory.
_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
**Dataset Description**

The dataset contains information about customers and their purchase behavior. Key variables include:

•	Customer ID – Unique identifier for each customer.

•	Gender – Male or Female.

•	Age – Age of the customer.

•	Country – Specific Country of Bike sold.

•	State – Location under every country.

•	Product_Category – Category type of every product sold of bikes.

•	Sub_Category – Type of which product sold.

•	Product -  Specific Name of the product.

•	Order_Quantity – How much quantity is sold to each customer.

•	Unit_Cost – Shows per unit cost.

•	Unit_Price – Shows per unit price of product.

•	Profit – Shows the Profit of total Quantity Purchased by every customer.

•	Cost – Total cost applied for every customer.

•	Revenue – Total selling price for every customer.

**Business Objectives**

1.	Identify customer segments most likely to purchase bikes.
2.	Analyze the impact of demographics (age, gender, product) on purchasing decisions.
3.	Evaluate how commute distance influences bike purchases.
4.	Support marketing and inventory strategies with data-driven insights.
________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
**Key Business Questions**

1.	Which age group contributes most to bike purchases?
2.	How does Product affect bike buying decisions?
3.	Which gender shows higher bike purchasing rates?
4.	How does commute distance influence the likelihood of purchasing a bike?
________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
**Tools & Techniques**

1.	Microsoft Excel, Power BI
2.	Data Cleaning using Power Query
3.	Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
4.	Conditional Formatting for key metrics visualization
5.	Formulas: SUMIFS, AVERAGEIFS, IFERROR, VLOOKUP, INDEX-MATCH
6.	Dynamic Dashboard creation using Slicers & Timelines
7.	Custom KPIs for customer segmentation and purchase trends
________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
**Deliverables**

1.	Interactive Power BI dashboard visualizing customer demographics and bike purchases.
2.	Pivot tables summarizing age, product, and gender distributions.
3.	Charts displaying bike purchases by age group, country & state, and commute distance.
4.	KPIs highlighting high-value customer segments and top-performing categories.
________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
**Key Insights**

1.	Middle-aged customers (31–45 years) are the most likely to purchase bikes.
2.	Higher Economic Country customers tend to buy more premium bikes.
3.	Coastal Region Citizens show a slightly higher purchase rate compared to singles.
4.	Male customers slightly outnumber female customers in bike ownership, but the gap is narrowing.
